Salad Recipe

Fresh ceasar salad

Classic Caesar Salad with crisp homemade croutons and a light caesar dressing for when you want to impress your dinner guests.

Ingredients

  • 1 large or 2 small heads of romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded or shaved
  • Crisp croutons
  • Caesar salad dressing

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine all of your ingredients and toss gently to coat the lettuce in caesar dressing. This recipe makes enough croutons for two full salads so you'll have them ready to go for round 2!
  2. Enjoy!
